My main focus and interest lies in Web development and associated technologies. While my languages of choice are JavaScript, PHP and Python, I'm willing to use/learn any language and associated technologies that best suite the problem being worked on.

About me

Download my CV (PDF)

I started programming in 6th grade, primary school. I worked in QBasic and went to a couple of city, regional and one state competition. In middle school I mostly programmed in Visual Basic and used HTML as part of our Informatics class.

In college I directed myself towards Web development. Started with PHP, then Python and then taught myself JavaScript. Also HTML, CSS, MySQL and MongoDB are a part of what I learned and still use.

My main working machine has been running Ubuntu since 2010. I've used Atom, NetBeans, Eclipse, Spyder and the Linux terminal as my work environments.

I like teaching people about open source software. At the Document Freedom Day 2015. event at the Department of Informatics I held a presentation [in Croatian] titled "My experience with transition from MS Office to LibreOffice" in which I also talked about open document formats, mainly OOXML vs. ODF.

In May 2015., I received the Rector's Award for Excellence as the best student of the Department of Informatics, University of Rijeka for the academic year 2014./2015., and in October, I received recognition from the municipality of Čavle for contributions in the development of education in 2015.

On September 24, 2015. I have successfully defended my Master's Thesis and obtained a Master's degree in Informatics.

Projects

Sleeping disorder research.

Connect-the-Words, educational game.

  • Build for a colleague assignment in Java. The goal of the game is to teach languages by connecting word translations.

Ditus, e-learning platform.

  • My assignment was the modification and application of the Whitelabel template by revaxarts, and the development of several backend modules. The application is build on the Phalcon PHP framework.
